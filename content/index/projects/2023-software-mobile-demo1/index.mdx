WarCardGame is an iOS mobile app developed as part of my self-learning journey in Swift, SwiftUI, and Xcode. This project encapsulates the fundamental concepts of mobile app development, showcasing my skills in creating engaging and interactive applications. The app features a simple yet captivating card game, designed to provide a hands-on experience with Swift programming and iOS app development principles. It represents my initial foray into the realm of iOS development, demonstrating both learning progress and practical application of new skills.
