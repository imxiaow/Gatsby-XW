This project involved designing and developing a comprehensive website for Molson Flooring & More, aimed at enhancing their digital brand presence. The focus was on creating a user-friendly interface with responsive design, ensuring seamless access across various devices. The project encompassed UX/UI design, highlighting the brand's services and value proposition effectively. The outcome was a visually appealing and functional website that improved Molson Flooring & More's online visibility and user engagement.
