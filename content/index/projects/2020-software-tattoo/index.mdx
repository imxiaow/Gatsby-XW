This project involved creating an online presence for Wild Crane Tattoos Inc., a high-end private tattoo studio. The focus was on designing a user-friendly, visually appealing website that reflected the studio's brand and values. Key aspects included a unique color palette, artist profiles, optimized content for SEO, and mobile responsiveness. The result was a significant increase in inquiries and appointments, highlighting the effectiveness of a well-designed website in driving business objectives and enhancing customer engagement in the tattoo industry.
